The Afrasian
Opportunity

Amidst the global resurgence of interest in Africa, often referred to as the last remaining frontier, Mauritius stands out as a vital International Finance Centre, drawing the attention of global entities seeking investment opportunities on the continent. With foreign investment pouring in from governments and businesses worldwide, the nation plays a pivotal role in facilitating these ventures. Accuvise, recognizing this potential, strategically establishes its headquarters on the Island of Mauritius, a prime International financial centre at the crossroads of Africa and Asia, perfectly positioned to serve as the ideal investment conduit in the region.
