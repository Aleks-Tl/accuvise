The Firm

Headquartered in the Island of Mauritus, Accuvise has purposefully positioned itself, both physically and strategically, in one of the most business friendly destinations on the globe. The Accuvise team is comprised of some of the most impactful, successful, and innovative financial growth and wealth management professionals in the industry, and as a team, represent several decades of combined experience and knowledge. With a commitment to compliance and the best practices of corporate governances, Accuvise is able to offer an array of attractive corporate, accounting, fiduciary, tax, private equity, and investment services that deliver exceptional results.

Why
Mauritius?

By choosing Mauritius as an investment hub, investors gain access to a stable, business-friendly environment with favourable tax incentives and investment protection. Its strategic location, skilled workforce, modern infrastructure, and participation in regional and international alliances make Mauritius an attractive and reliable destination for expanding global operations and tapping into emerging markets.Accuvise Administrators Ltd has set its headquarters on the Island of Mauritius,known as the pearl of the Indian ocean, for its strategic location at the crossroads of Africa and Asia.

  • Strategic Location

    Mauritius' strategic location at the crossroads of Africa and Asia makes it a strategic investment gateway to both continents. Investors can tap into a vast and diverse market, accessing opportunities in emerging economies.

  • Business-Friendly Environment

    Mauritius is renowned for its business-friendly environment, with policies that encourage foreign investment. The government actively promotes foreign direct investment (FDI) and has streamlined regulatory processes, making it easy to set up and operate businesses.

  • Political Stability

    Mauritius boasts a long history of political stability, with a transparent and efficient legal system that upholds the rule of law. Investors can rely on a stable and predictable business environment, minimising political risks.

  • Tax Advantages

    The country offers various tax incentives for businesses. Mauritius has entered into several Double Taxation Avoidance Agreements (DTAs) with numerous countries, providing investors with tax benefits and eliminating the risk of double taxation.

  • Investment Protection

    Mauritius provides a robust legal framework that protects foreign investments. It is a signatory to various international conventions and treaties, safeguarding investors' interests and offering recourse in case of disputes.

  • Financial Services Expertise

    Mauritius is a well-established financial services hub, with a sophisticated and well-regulated financial sector. It offers a wide range of financial services, including fund management, banking, insurance, and private equity.

  • Currency Convertibility

    The Mauritian Rupee is freely convertible, allowing investors to repatriate profits and funds without restrictions. This flexibility simplifies international transactions and enhances the ease of doing business.

  • Skilled Workforce

    Mauritius boasts a highly educated and multilingual workforce. English and French are widely spoken, making it conducive for businesses to operate and communicate seamlessly.

  • Modern Infrastructure

    Mauritius has modern infrastructure, including state-of-the-art telecommunications, transportation, and technology. This supports efficient business operations and connectivity with global markets.

  • Economic and Political Alliances

    Mauritius is a member of various economic and political alliances, such as the African Union, Southern African Development Community (SADC), and the Common Market for Eastern and Southern Africa (COMESA). These memberships provide access to a wide network of markets and facilitate regional trade and cooperation.
